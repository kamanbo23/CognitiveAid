    Our Efforts

    We are committed to developing tools that enhance the quality of life for seniors and individuals with cognitive disabilities. The developmental process began when Lokesh's neighbor who had raised him all his life was diagnosed with alzheimer's. As he forgot his daily routine, Lokesh sought to help and give back to the man who had help raise him. The reminders feature was the first feature in this applicaiton. This app was then used by people around the neighborhood suffering from memory loss. Lokesh took this rising popularity as a means to implement more features in order help a larger range of neurodivergent individuals. This app is being integrated into camps that work with neurodivergent individuals or older members who desire aid.
